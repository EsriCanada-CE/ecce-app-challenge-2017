Instructions

The main purpose of this web application is to estimate the carbon footprint.

By using this App, the user can estimate the carbon footprint depending on the car model and distance travelled.

Main widgets are located at the center bottom of the web.


1. Select your car model from the widget below to compute the Fuel Economy (Miles per Gallon).
2. Using the Travel Distance widget, enter the desired destination from the starting point (Miles).
3. Note that the average Passenger car has carbon emission factor of 0.355.
4. Enter the Fuel Economy and Travel distance into the Carbon Calculator.

Links are provided for consequences of carbon emissions for humans.


Depending on the user preference, the traffic layer can be removed by using the swipe widget located on the right top corner.
