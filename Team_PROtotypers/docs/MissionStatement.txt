Mission Statement

This application allows a user to find the nearest safe bike lockup area from a recreational facility in the city of Waterloo. With a way to easily find the closest safe bike lockup area to reduce the risk of a cyclists bike being stolen, Team PROtotypers hopes to increase ridership and use of sustainable transportation methods.
